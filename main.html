About Toy Story

Toy Story is a beloved Pixar Animation Studios franchise that began in 1995 as the first fully computer-animated feature film. It explores themes of friendship, loyalty, and growing up through the adventures of toys that come to life when humans aren't around.

First Animated Feature

Toy Story was the first fully computer-animated feature film, revolutionizing animation.

Iconic Characters

Featuring beloved characters like Woody and Buzz Lightyear, who have become cultural icons.

Academy Award Winner

The franchise has won multiple Academy Awards, including Best Original Screenplay for the first film.

Movies

Toy Story Movies

Toy Story (1995)

The original film introducing Woody and Buzz Lightyear as they embark on an adventure to find their way back to their owner, Andy.

Toy Story 2 (1999)

Woody is kidnapped by a toy collector, and Buzz and the gang must rescue him before he's sold to a museum in Japan.

Toy Story 3 (2010)

Andy is heading to college, and the toys face an uncertain future at a daycare center, leading to new adventures and heartwarming moments.

Toy Story 4 (2019)

Woody sets out on a road trip with Bo Peep, while Buzz and the others deal with a new toy named Forky, exploring themes of change and belonging.

Characters

Featured Characters

Woody

The loyal pull-string cowboy doll who is the leader of the toys, known for his bravery and sense of responsibility.

Buzz Lightyear

The high-tech space ranger action figure who believes he's a real space hero, bringing excitement and adventure to the group.

Jessie

The spunky cowgirl doll with a yodeling personality, who loves adventure and has a heart full of courage and independence.

Bo Peep

The elegant porcelain shepherdess who is wise, kind, and adventurous, often guiding the toys with her gentle leadership.

Rex

The friendly T-Rex dinosaur toy who is gentle despite his appearance, loves video games, and is part of the toy gang.

Forky

A spork turned into a toy by Bonnie, who struggles with his identity but becomes a loyal friend to Woody and the others.
